Chinese Culture and Customs

Cultural Food

There is a wide range of different foods native to various regions in China. Because the country is so large, many provinces within China have their own unique cultural foods. However, some of the more common dishes are shared all over the country, and many of these have also become popular in Western culture. You can learn more here.

Here are some examples:

  • Hotpot
  • Dim Sum
  • Dumplings
  • Chow Mein
  • Fried Rice
  • Peking Duck
  • Green Onion Cake
  • Wonton Soup
